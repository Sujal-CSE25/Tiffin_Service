GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pause my subscription? ▼
Yes! You can pause your subscription anytime from your dashboard. Just click "Pause Meals" and your subscription will be paused. You can resume whenever you want with no extra charges.
What is the order cutoff time? ▼
Lunch orders must be placed before 7:00 AM. Dinner orders must be placed before 7:00 PM. Orders placed after cutoff will be scheduled for the next day automatically.
Is the food pure vegetarian? ▼
Yes! We are 100% pure vegetarian. Our kitchen is FSSAI certified and we do not use any non-vegetarian ingredients, onion, or garlic in our preparations.
Can I customize my tiffin? ▼
Yes, you can add special instructions for your order. We accommodate dietary restrictions like no spice, diabetic-friendly meals, and jain food on request.
What areas do you deliver to? ▼
We currently deliver to Faizabad, Ayodhya, and surrounding areas. Enter your pincode during checkout to check delivery availability in your area.

OUR BEGINNING

From Our Kitchen,
With Love Since 2019

Saumya Tiffin Service was born out of a simple desire — to make sure working professionals, students, and families far from home always have access to wholesome, homemade food.

Our founder, Smt. Saumya Devi, started cooking in her small kitchen for 5 families in 2019. Today, we serve over 2,400 happy families across the region, but our heart remains the same — every meal is cooked with the love of a mother.

We use only the freshest vegetables sourced from local farms, traditional spices, and recipes passed down through generations. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure ghar ka khana.
